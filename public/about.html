About Noema

How It Works

Noema is an AI-powered guessing game powered by Claude Opus 4.5, Anthropic's most advanced language model. Think of anyone - a celebrity, a friend, yourself, a trader, a fictional character - and I'll try to guess who you're thinking of by asking strategic yes/no questions.

Technical Details

  • AI Model: Claude Opus 4.5 (claude-opus-4-5-20251101)
  • Question Strategy: Adaptive questioning that learns from your answers
  • Learning System: Tracks wrong guesses and correct answers to improve over time
  • Response Time: Optimized for speed with limited tokens and conversation history

Question Strategy

I start by asking about gender, then vary my questions across different topics:

  • Appearance (hair color, height, distinctive features)
  • Relationships and personal life
  • Time period and nationality
  • Achievements and hobbies
  • Media presence and distinctive characteristics

I learn from your answers - if you say "Don't know" to a question about age, I won't ask similar age-related questions. If you say someone is bald, I'll make sure my guess matches that detail.

Learning & Improvement

This is a new version and may occasionally guess incorrectly. With more user interactions and feedback, I'll continue to learn and improve over time. Your patience helps me get better!

I track:

  • Wrong guesses to learn what not to guess
  • Correct answers to understand patterns
  • Question patterns to avoid repetition
  • Exclusions from "no" answers to avoid contradicting guesses
